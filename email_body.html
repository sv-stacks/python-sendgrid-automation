Hi there,

Over the past few months, we haven’t talked much about new features. That’s not because nothing was happening — on the contrary. We’ve been working behind the scenes, focusing on maximizing the stability of all core features.

We rebuilt the streaming, rewrote libraries, and improved a lot under the hood — all to make live shoots perform reliably in real conditions, not just when the internet is perfect. And the results are already noticeable.

Now it’s simple to see: the stream stays stable even when the connection drops. The image quality may dip a little and then recover, but the stream itself never breaks. This is especially important during paid live shoots. And when the internet is good, the video becomes crystal-clear, almost 4K.

One of the most striking moments recently was a shoot in Italy. Underground parking — a notoriously unstable connection. Ferrari, Lamborghini, two stunning models, and pro photographer Francesco (BlueFashion Photography). Usually, streams would freeze and buffer in these conditions… but the shoot just went on. No interruptions. Attendees could watch, participate, and stay in the moment. For us, that was a moment of truth — proof that all the work was worth it.

There’s no magic — you still need some internet. But if it’s there, even at normal mobile speeds, the platform takes over and keeps things running smoothly. Our tests even show we’ve surpassed some tech giants in streaming stability, and we’re proud of that.

Now that this foundation is in place, we’re moving forward:
• Preparing a super-subscription for active event creators
• Creating a dedicated space for models on the main screen (many top models already trust the platform)
• Making it easier to see their work and interact with it

We’ll share more details soon.
